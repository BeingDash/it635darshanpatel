A little something about how I came up with this project.

Lunch Management System or LMS as I like to call it was created by Darshan Patel for IT635 Class Project.

I work for a small to midsize company in Teaneck, NJ. They provide free lunch for all the employees everyday. The way ordering works right now is that the receptionist picks a restaurant for the day and passes around a piece of paper and everyone writes what they want to order. The receptionist then scans the documents and emails it to the restaurant.

This works, but its really time consuming and its a hassle to pass around the paper and store the historical records.

Hence I came up with an idea to create a Lunch Management System which would allow user to Login and place an order. The information would then be stored in a database.
